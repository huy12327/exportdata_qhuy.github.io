/drɪl/

The man cut his woodstick with a hacksaw.

 Utility knife

/juːˈtɪl.ə.ti naɪf/

 Measuring tape

/ˈmeʒ.əriŋ teɪp/

 Magnifier

/ˈmæɡnɪfaɪər/

A magnifier can make things look larger.

 Scissors

/ˈsɪz.əz/

Cái kéo


Could you pass the the scissors, please.

 Soldering iron

/ˈsəʊl.də.rɪŋ ˌaɪən/

I use a soldering iron to solder the wires.

 Blade

/bleɪd/

lưỡi (dao, kiếm)

 Handle

/ˈhændəl/

(n) Phần tay cầm (của một dụng cụ)

 Shaft

/ʃɑːft/

This shaft is made of metal.

 Jaw

/dʒɔː/

(n) hàm


The pliers' jaw is hardened.

 Bit

/bɪt/

Mũi (khoan)


I need a bigger drill bit.

 Tip

/tɪp/

 Bore

/bɔːr/

He used a drill to bore a hole in the wall.

/ˈtaɪ.tən/

 Loosen

/heksˈæɡənl/
