 Identify

/aɪˈdentɪfaɪ/

Nhận biết, xác định


You have to check the CPU to identify the faults.

 Examine

/ɪɡˈzæmɪn/

Kiểm tra, khảo sát


We need to examine how well this machine works.

 Confirm

/kənˈfɜːm/

Xác nhận


Please confirm your order that you made on last Monday.

 Assume

/əˈsjuːm/

Giả định/cho rằng


To assume is to think or accept that something is true but without having proof of it.

 Manufacture

/ˌmænjuˈfæktʃə(r)/

Sản xuất


To manufacture something is to make goods in large quantities, using machinery.

 Manufacturer

/ˌmænjuˈfæktʃərə(r)/

Nhà sản xuất


HP is one of the most famous computer manufacturers in the world.

 Procedure

/prəˈsiːdʒə(r)/

Quy trình


The procedure for logging on to the network usually involves a password.

 Remark

/rɪˈmɑːk/

Lời nhận xét, bình luận


The technician gives me some remarks about how to solve the problem.

 Possible

/ˈpɒsɪb(ə)l/

Khả thi


Here is a list of possible answers. Please choose the one that suits the most.

 Possibility

/ˌpɒsəˈbɪləti/

Khả năng


Possibility is the fact that something might exist or happen, but is not certain to

 desolder

/dɪˈsəʊldə(r)/

Tháo mối hàn


Desoldering is to remove the solder and components from a circuit board.

 come into contact with

/kʌm ˈɪntə ˈkɒntækt wɪð/

Tiếp xúc với cái gì


Do not let your skin come into contact with the acid.

 Expose to

/ɪkˈspəʊz tə/

Tiếp xúc


Don’t expose yourself to fire.

 Injury

/ˈɪndʒəri/

Tổn thương


He fell off and had a head injury.

 Injured

/ˈɪndʒəd/

Bị tổn thương


Ambulances took the injured man to a nearby hospital

 Burn

/bɜːn/

Vết bỏng/Bỏng


Burn is an injury or a mark caused by fire, heat or acid. If you burn a fuel, it produces heat, light or energy

 fatal

/ˈfeɪtl/

(adj) chí tử


This illness is fatal in almost all cases.

 Fatality

fəˈtæl.ə.ti/

Cái chết


There are thousands of electrical fatalities (= deaths in electrical accidents) every year.

 Convert

/kənˈ'vɜːt/

Chuyển đổi


How do you convert moles into grams?

 Corrode

/kəˈrəʊd/

Ăn mòn


Acid corrodes metal.

 Corrosion

/kəˈrəʊʒn/

Sự ăn mòn


There was a lot of corrosion on the bottom of the car

 Damage

/ˈdæmɪdʒ/

Thiệt hại, hư hại


The library suffered bad damage in the fire.

 Failure

/ˈfeɪljə(r)/

(n) lack of success in doing or achieving something


The success or failure of the plan depends on you.

 defect

/ˈdiːfekt/

Lỗi


The camera contains serious defects.

 Internal

/ɪnˈtɜːnl/

Thuộc về bên trong


I have to check the internal parts of this CPU to identify the problem.

 Effectively

/ɪˈfektɪvli/

Một cách hiệu quả


If you install this software, your computer certainly works effectively

 Appropriately/ properly

/əˈprəʊpriətli/ /ˈprɒpəli/

Một cách phù hợp


The television isn't working properly

 Retrieve

/rɪˈtriːv/

Lấy, gọi ra (thông tin)


Using this program helps retrieve information from the database.

 Be in operation

/bi ɪn ˌɒpəˈreɪʃn/

Hoạt động/vận hành


The system has been in operation for six months.
