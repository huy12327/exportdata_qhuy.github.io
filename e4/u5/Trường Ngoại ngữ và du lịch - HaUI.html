 Robot

/ˈrəʊ.bɒt/

người máy


The surgery can be carried out by robots.

 Robotics

/rəʊˈbɒt.ɪks/

Khoa học nghiên cứu việc sử dụng người máy


Robotics is an interesting science.

 Artificial intelligence

/ˌɑː.tɪ.fɪʃ.əl ɪnˈtel.ɪ.dʒəns/

Trí tuệ nhân tạo


There have been many movies about artificial intelligence.

 Application

/ˌæplɪˈkeɪʃn/

ứng dụng, app


This mobile phone can store many applications

 Sensor

/ˈsɛnsə/

Cảm biến


The security device has a heat sensor which detects the presence of people and animals.

 Automated

/ˈɔt̬əˌmeɪt̬ɪd/

Được tự động hóa


This part of the assembly process is now fully automated.

 Mimic

/ˈmɪm.ɪk/

Bắt chước


This robot can mimic human facial muscles.

 Interact

/ˌɪntərˈækt/

tương tác


The only thing he interacts with is his computer

 Improve

/ɪmˈpruːv/

Cải thiện


Many scientists have tried to improve the efficiency of this vacuum robot.

 Benefit

/ˈbenɪfɪt/

Lợi ích (n)


Robots bring a lot of benefit to humankinds.

 Positive aspect

/ˈpɒz.ə.tɪv ˈæs.pekt/

Mặt tích cực


There are many positive aspects of robots.

 Good point

/ɡʊd pɔɪnt/

Ưu điểm


One good point of this robot is that it can move around objects easily.

 Drawback

/ˈdrɔː.bæk/

Nhược điểm (n)


One drawback of solar energy is dependent on the weather.

 Replace

/rɪˈpleɪs/

Thay thế


This robot is created to replace vacuum cleaner.

 Perform

/pəˈfɔːm/

Thực hiện


This robot can perform different tasks at the same time.

 Recognize (Recognized - Part simple)

/ˈrek.əɡ.naɪz/

Nhận diện


Recognizing voices and faces is a common ability of modern robots.

 Protect

/prəˈtekt/

bảo vệ


This robot can help protect your house when you are away.

 serve

/sɜ:v/

(v) phục vụ

 Affordable

/əˈfɔː.də.bəl/

(giá cả) phải chăng, có thể chấp nhận được


May boutique has many nice clothes at affordable prices for students.

 Detect

/dɪˈtekt/

This robot’s sensor can detect the position of objects.

 Command

/kəˈmænd/

Robots are made to take commands from humans.

 Processor

/ˈprəʊsesə(r)/

 controller

/kənˈtrəʊlə(r)/

 wheel

/wiːl/

This robot runs on four wheels.

An actuator is a device that converts energy into physical motion.
