 Apologize for

/əˈpɒl.ə.dʒaɪz fɔː(r)/

Xin lỗi


She didn’t apologize for breaking my new bulb.

 Attach

/əˈtætʃ/

Gắn kèm, đính kèm


I have attached a photo below my complaint letter.

 Complain

/kəmˈpleɪn/

Phàn nàn


I would like to complain about the printer I bought from you.

 complaint

/kəmˈpleɪnt/

Sự phàn nàn


I want to make a complaint about a DVD player.

 Contact

/ˈkɒntækt/

(v) Liên lạc


You can contact me on 388 9194.

 Fault

/fɒlt/

Lỗi


I think it has an electrical fault.

 Faulty

/ˈfɒl.ti/

Có lỗi


We’ll collect the faulty player from you.

 Inconvenience

/ˌɪnkənˈviːniəns/

Sự bất tiện


We are sorry for any inconvenience.

 Package

/ˈpakɪdʒ/

Đóng gói


A large package has arrived for you .

 Pleased

/pliːzd/

Hài lòng, vừa lòng


I'm pleased to tell you that we’ll replace the adapter for you.

 Reasonable

/ˈriː.zənəbl ̩/

Hợp lý


We’re going to give you 5 euros discount off the next purchase. - That’s very reasonable.

 Reduce the price

/rɪˈdjuːs/ /ðə/ /praɪs/

Giảm giá


We will reduce the price on your next purchase.

 refund

/ˈriːfʌnd/

Hoàn tiền


They refunded me the price of the TV.

 Refund (n)

/ˈriː:fʌnd/

Tiền trả lại


I'm afraid it isn't our policy to give refunds, sir.

 Return (n)

/rɪˈtɜːn/

Việc gửi trả lại hàng, Gửi trả lại hàng


This new machine is not working so I will return it to the supermarket.

 replacement

/rɪˈpleɪsm(ə)nt/

Sự thay thế


You’ll receive the replacement by the end of next week.

 resolve

/rɪˈzɒlv/

Giải quyết


We’ll resolve the problem as soon as possible.

 Troubleshooting tips

/ˈtrʌb.l ̩ʃuː.tɪŋ/ /tɪps/

Mẹo xử lí sự cố


I have tried all of the troubleshooting tips in the manual.

 Bend

/bend/

Bẻ cong


A leg of the table is bent

 Blank (screen)

/blæŋk skrin /

(Màn hình) trắng (do không hiển thị được hình ảnh)


Something is wrong with my computer. Its screen is blank.

 Crush

/krʌʃ/

Đè nát, làm nhàu


Look! The handle (quai vali/ tay cầm) is crushed.

 Compatible (with)

/kəmˈpæt.ɪ.bl ̩/

Tương thích


This copier is not compatible with my computer.

 Dent

/dent/

Làm lõm/ vết lõm


The top of my case is dented.

 Flicker

/ˈflɪk.ə r /

Lập lòe


The light flickered and turned off.

 Flickering

/ˈflɪk. ə r.ɪŋ/

Lập lòe, lung linh


The laptop screen is always flickering, so I will bring it to the repair store.

 jam

/dʒæm/

(n) sự tắc nghẽn


We were stuck in a jam for an hour.

 jammed

/dʒæmd/

Bị kẹt


Look at my suitcase! The lock (khóa) is jammed.

 Hole

/həʊl/

Cái lỗ


There is a hole in my new table.

 Missing (part)

/ˈmɪs.ɪŋ/

Thiếu bộ


I’m calling you to ask about the missing part of the fridge.

 Scratch

/skrætʃ/

Làm xước


The top surface (mặt trên) is badly scratched.

 Stuck

/stʌk/

Bị tắc


The lock seems to be stuck. Can you help me to open the door?
