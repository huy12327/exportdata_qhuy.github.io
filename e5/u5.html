 Maintain

/meɪnˈteɪn/

bảo trì, bảo dưỡng


You should maintain your washing machine every 6 months.

 maintenance

/ˈmeɪnt(ə)nəns/

Sự bảo trì, bảo dưỡng


Regular maintenance helps your AC work well and productively.

 Lubricate

/ˈluːbrɪkeɪt/

(v) tra dầu, bôi trơn


You need to lubricate the moving parts regularly.

 Dismantle

/dɪsˈmæntl/

(v) tháo rời, tháo ra


You should dismantle the water hose filter to wash it.

 Overhaul

/ˈəʊvəhɔːl/

(v) đại tu


You ought to overhaul your air conditioner every year.

 Wear out

/weə(r) aʊt/

(v) mòn


Moving parts of machine often wear out.

 Tear

/teə(r)/

xé, làm rách


to damage something by pulling it apart or into pieces or by cutting it on something sharp Ex: I tore a hole in my jeans.

 Blow

/bləʊ/

(v) nổ, cháy


If the fuse blows, the electricity stops flowing suddenly./ My air conditioner is blowing but not cooling.

 Detergent

/dɪˈtɜːdʒənt/

(n) xà phòng, chất tẩy rửa


You had better use mild detergent to clean it.

 Hose

/həʊz/

(n) ống


The inlet hose filter may be dirty and is blocking the water.

 Cycle

/ˈsaɪkl/

(n) chu kì làm việc, chu trình làm việc


Determine the correct load for each cycle for your washing machine.

 Kink

/kɪŋk/

(v) thắt, uốn gập, vặn


Don't kink the water hose.

 Water tap

/ˈwɔːtə(r) tæp/

(n) vòi nước


Make sure to open the water tap for the washing machine.

 Clog

/klɒɡ/

(v) kẹt, tắc,lấp kín


Dust and dirt can clog the hose.

 Drain

/dreɪn/

(v) xả nước, thoát nước


Water drains slowly because the hose is clogged.

 Laundry load

/ˈlɔːndri ləʊd /

(n) mẻ giặt


Weigh the laundry load to add the right amount of detergent.

 Coil

/kɔɪl/

(n) cuộn dây


The coils leak a lot, you had better replace them.

 Fin

/fɪn/

(n) cánh tản nhiệt


The fin can get clogged with dirt and debris.

 Refrigerant (n) = coolant (n)

/rɪˈfrɪdʒərənt/ =/ˈkuːlənt/

môi chất làm lạnh


The technician is recharging the refrigerant for the AC. The coolant is leaking, you should repair the leakage immediately

 Compressor

/kəmˈpresə(r)/

(n) máy nén


A compressor is responsible for cooling your room.

 Thermostat

/ˈθɜːməstæt/

(n) bộ điều nhiệt


A thermostat can often be the main control unit for a heating or cooling system.

 Condenser

/kənˈdensə(r)/

(n) bình ngưng, dàn ngưng, thiết bị ngưng


A condenser is one of the key components of the AC system.

 Condensate

/ˌkɒndenˈseɪt/

(n) nước ngưng, phần ngưng tụ


Condensate problems can lead to leaks into the building.

 Sealant

/ˈsiːlənt/

(n) chất bít, chất trám


You can use the sealant to repair the leakage from small hole.

 Vent

/vent/

(n) cửa thông gió


The air vent should be cleaned every six months.

 Duct

/dʌkt/

(n) đường dây, đường ống cáp


Air flows through the ventilation ducts.

 Furnace

/ˈfɜːnɪs/

(n) lò, hơi nóng, khí nóng


It's so hot.It's just like a furnace in here!

 Breaker

/ˈbreɪkə(r)/

(n) bộ ngắt điện


Make sure the circuit breaker works well.

 radiator

/ˈreɪdieɪtə(r)/

(n) bộ tản nhiệt


A radiator is a part of a cooling system.

 Blower

/ˈbləʊə(r)/

(n) quạt thông gió


Fans and blowers have to be designed properly.

 Absorb

/əbˈzɔːb/ or /əbˈsɔːb/

(v) hấp thụ


Your air conditioner absorbs heat from the air and releases it outside.

 Circulate

/ˈsɜːkjəleɪt/

(v) lưu thông, tuần hoàn


The AC also circulates the air from your home through a vent.

 Evaporator

/ɪˈvæpəˌreɪtə/

(n) dàn bay hơi, dàn lạnh


The evaporator removes heat from the area that is to be cooled.

 Rinse

/rɪns/

(v) súc, rửa


You could rinse the air conditioner coil with the water hose.

 Stain (n)

/steɪn/

vết bẩn


How can I clean the stain on this shirt?
