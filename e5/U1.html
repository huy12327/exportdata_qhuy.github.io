/faʊnd/

The college was founded in 1872.

/kɔːpəˈreɪʃ(ə)n/

(n) Tập đoàn


Samsung is a Korean corporation that designs and manufactures electronic products.

/ˈɡræfɪks ˈprəʊsesɪŋ ˈjuːnɪt/

/həʊm əˈplaɪəns/

/ˈɪnəveɪtɪv/

(adj) Có tính đổi mới


The Canon Lab was established to encourage innovative thinking for new customer solutions.

 Mission

/ˈmɪʃn/

(n) Sứ mệnh, nhiệm vụ


Google's mission is to organize the world's information and make it accessible and useful.

 Multinational

/ˌmʌltiˈnæʃnəl/

/ˌɒpəˈreɪʃn/

 Revenue

/ˈrevənjuː/

/ˈseɡmənt/

/semikənˈdʌktə(r)/

/kənˈtrɪbjuːt/

Panasonic is continously contributing to the society and the happiness of people.

 Cooperation

/kəʊɒpəˈreɪʃn/

Facebook focuses on cooperation and open communication.

/kəʊˈɒpəreɪt/

/kriˈeɪt/

/ˌkriːeɪˈtɪvəti/
