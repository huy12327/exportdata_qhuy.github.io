 Device

/dɪˈvʌɪs/

Thiết bị


The room is equipped with new devices.

 Lighter

/ˈlʌɪtə/

Bật lửa


She pulled out a small silver lighter from her bag.

 Compass

/ˈkʌmpəs/

La bàn


People need compasses to check directions.

 Remote control

/rɪˈməʊt kənˈtrəʊl/

Điều khiển


I can't find the remote control of the TV.

 Torch

/tɔːtʃ/

Đèn pin


We need a torch when walking in the dark.

 Stove

/stəʊv/

Bếp lò


Stay far from the stove, it's hot.

 Printer

/ˈprɪntə(r)/

Máy in


The printer is out of order.

 Speakers

/ˈspiːkə/

Loa


Can you bring a speaker tomorrow?

 Channel

/ˈtʃan(ə)l/

Kênh (Tivi)


I like watching programmes on Discovery Channel.

 Blender

/ˈblɛndə/

Máy xay sinh tố


You can use a blender to make smoothies.

 Manual

/ˈmanjʊ(ə)l/

Sách hướng dẫn sử dụng


Make sure you read the manual before using the oven.

 Specification

/ˌspɛsɪfɪˈkeɪʃ(ə)n/

Thông số


The house has been built exactly to our specifications.

 Button

/ˈbʌt(ə)n/

Nút (trên thiết bị)


There are so many buttons on a remote control.

 Press

/prɛs/

Nhấn


Press the red button to stop the device.

 Rewind

/riːˈwʌɪnd/

Tua lại


Could you rewind the tape?

 pause/ stop

/pɔːz/ stɒp/

Dừng lại


She paused the DVD and went to answer the phone.

 Increase/ decrease

/ɪnˈkriːs/ dɪˈkriːs/

Tăng/giảm


Last year, the price of oil increased for nearly 50%.

 Record

/rɪˈkɔːd/

Thu âm


Tell me when the tape starts recording.

 Fast forward

/fɑːst ˈfɔːwəd/

tua (băng đĩa)


Press fast forward to advance the tape.

 Turn on/ turn off

/tɜːrn ɔːn/ tɜːrnɔːf/

Bật/tắt


Turn on the fan please! It’s so hot in there.

 Turn up/turn down

/tɜːrn ʌp/ /tɜːrn daʊn/

Tăng/giảm (Âm lượng)


Turn down the volume, please!
