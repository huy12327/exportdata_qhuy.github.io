 Charge

/tʃɑːdʒ/

nạp điện


He charged the mobile phone.

 Discharge

/dɪsˈtʃɑːdʒ/

không nạp điện


He discharged the battery.

 Safety

/ˈseɪfti/

an toàn, chỗ an toàn


You should play in a safety place.

 Conductive

/kənˈdʌktɪv/

dẫn điện


Copper is a common electrically conductive material.

 Hazard

/ˈhæzəd/

sự nguy hiểm


Everybody must be aware of the safety hazards.

 Risk

/rɪsk/

sự nguy hiểm, sự liều, sự mạo hiểm


The benefits outweigh the risks.

 Cover / uncover

/ˈkʌvə(r) // ʌnˈkʌvə(r)/

Che, phủ, bao phủ, bao trùm, bao bọc/ không che


She covered her face with her hands.

Archaeologists have uncovered an entire Roman city.

 Non-conductive

/nɔn kənˈdʌktɪv/

không dẫn điện


Glass, plastic and rubber are examples of non-conductive materials.

 Electrocution

/ɪˌlektrəˈkjuːʃn/

Sự bị điện giật chết


Six people were drowned; five died from electrocution.

 De-energize

/di:´enə¸dʒaiz/

ngắt điện, giảm năng


They de-energized the whole city because of electricity accident.

 Warning

/ˈwɔːnɪŋ/

Lời cảnh cáo, cảnh báo


The bridge collapsed without (any) warnings.

 Rubber

/ˈrʌbə/

cao su


Tom put on some rubber gloves so that he wouldn"t leave fingerprints.

 Helmet/ hard hat

/ˈhelmɪt/ hɑːrd hæt/

mũ cứng


It is compulsory to wear a helmet while riding motorbike.

 Chemical

/ˈkemɪkl/

Chất hoá học; hoá chất


Farmers are being urged to reduce their use of chemicals.

 Sole

/səʊl/

đế giày


The soles of my shoes are worn.

 Protection

/prəˈtekʃn/

sự bảo vệ


Wear clothes that provide adequate protection against the wind and rain.

 container

/kənˈteɪnə(r)/

bình chứa


Food will last longer if kept in an airtight container.

 glove

/ɡlʌv/

găng tay


You must wear rubber gloves before going to the laboratory .

 Shock

/ʃɒk/

sự sốc


  • An electric shock occurs when a person comes into contact with an electrical energy source.

 Face mask

/feɪs mɑːsk/

mặt nạ


Wearing face masks protects workers’ face from heat.

 Ignore

/ɪɡˈnɔː(r)/

Lờ đi, phớt đi, phớt lờ


He ignored all the ‘No Smoking’ signs and lit up a cigarette.

 Slippery

/ˈslɪpəri/

trơn, khó nắm giữ, khó đứng, khó đi


In some places, the path can be wet and slippery.

 Come into contact with/ touch

/kʌm ˈɪntə ˈkɒntækt wɪð/ tʌtʃ/

đụng chạm


Do not let your skin come into contact with the acid.

 Safety guard coat

/ˈseɪfti ɡɑːrd kəʊt/

áo bảo hộ


Every worker has to wear safety guard coats when they are at the construction site.

 Apron

/ˈeɪprən/

Cái tạp dề


When you are cooking, you should wear an apron.

 Live connection point

/lɪv kəˈnekʃn pɔɪnt/

điểm tiếp nối có dòng điện chạy qua


A pair of live connection points are needed for a circuit.

 Live wire

/lɪv ˈwaɪə(r)/

dây nóng


A live wire had connected with the metal bars of the house.

 Retain

/rɪˈteɪn/

Giữ lại


He struggled to retain control of the situation.

 Flammable

/ˈflæməbl/

Bén lửa, dễ cháy


These materials are highly flammable.

 Obstacle

/ˈɒbstəkl/

Sự cản trở, sự trở ngại


A lack of qualifications can be a major obstacle to finding a job.
