 Broken

/ˈbrəʊkən/

Bị gãy, bị vỡ, bị hỏng


My watch is broken

 Burnt out (bulb)

/bɜːrnt/ /aʊt/

(Bị) cháy (đèn)


Three bulbs in my house burned out yesterday.

 Cracked

/krækt/

Bị vỡ


My iPhone screen was cracked.

 Dead (monitor)

/ded/

Bị chết (màn hình)


The monitor of his computer is dead.

 Far from the socket

/fɑːr frəm ðə ˈsɑːkɪt/

Xa ổ cắm


My bed is far from the socket.

 Flat (battery)

/flæt/

Hết pin


The battery of my clock is flat.

 Freeze

/friːz/

Đơ máy, treo máy


My computer freezes again.

 Leak

/liːk/

Rò rỉ


The water is leaking in the bathroom.

 Loose

/luːs/

Lỏng


There’s a loose connection in the plug.

 Missing

/ˈmɪsɪŋ/

Thiếu


An important program in your computer is missing.

 Driver

/ˈdraɪvə(r)/

Ổ đĩa


The computer isn’t working because there is no driver.

 Program

/ˈproʊɡræm/

Chương trình


You can’t download videos because there is no program to do it.

 Work

/wɜːrk/

Làm việc, hoạt động


<p>My phone isn’t working. </p>

 Out of order

/aʊt əvˈɔːrdər/

Bị hỏng


This refrigerator doesn’t work. It’s out of order.

 Overheat

/ˌəʊvəˈhiːt/

Quá nóng


My phone is overheating. I’ve been using it for 9 hours.

 Run out of (battery)

/rʌn aʊt əv/

Hết (pin)


Your phone will run out of battery soon. You’ve been playing games for 3 hours.

 Slow performance

/sloʊ pərˈfɔːrməns/

Vận hành chậm


Too many programs running at the same time can cause slow performance.

 Terminal

['tə:minl]

Mối nối, đầu nối


There is a problem with the terminal.

 Wiring problem

/ˈwaɪərɪŋ ˈprɑːbləm/

Vấn đề về nối dây


I think this bulb has a wiring problem.

 Ask for technical support

/æsk fərˈteknɪkl səˈpɔːrt/

Yêu cầu hỗ trợ kỹ thuật


You should ask for technical support with this kind of problem.

 Charge

/tʃɑːdʒ/

Sạc


This laptop needs to be charged right away.

 Check the connection

/tʃek ðə kəˈnekʃn/

Kiểm tra kết nối


If you can"t turn the computer on, check the connection between the cord and the plug.

 Check the fan

/tʃek ðə fæn/

Kiểm tra quạt ( máy tính)


You should check the fan when the computer overheats.

 Do a back-up

/du ə bæk ʌp/

Sao lưu


It is very important to do a back-up before you reinstall this program.

 Install

/ɪnˈstɔːl/

Cài đặt


You should install an antivirus program to protect your computer.

 Plug into

/plʌɡ ˈɪntə/

Cắm


She plugs the charger into the socket.

 Replace

/rɪˈpleɪs/

Thay thế


You should replace this bulb.

 Take precautions

/teɪk prɪˈkɔːʃn/

Thực hiện các biện pháp phòng ngừa


We always take precautions when working.

 Use a clamp

/juːz ə klæmp/

Sử dụng kẹp


You can use a clamp to hold things together.

 Use a tape

/juːz ə teɪp/

Sử dụng băng


You can use a tape to do many things.

 Use an extension lead

/juːz ən ɪkˈstenʃn liːd/

Sử dụng ổ cắm kéo dài


When the socket is too far from the bed, you can use an extension lead.

 Use a checklist

/juːz əˈtʃeklɪst/

Sử dụng danh sách kiểm tra


When a problem happens to your computer, you can use a checklist to find out what’s wrong.
